## User dashboard contains

In that space we reunite all you can do in yaPCP.  

### Personal contact info

In yaPcp we maintain separate access data w/email n password
from all your personal infos, so after first login we suggest you
to open and update immediately your personal info contact data.

That form require the data that we share with contest organizer, and
sponsor federation, data you usually need to reinsert every, every
and every time participate a photo contest. Some infos are required
from national and international photo organization.

- [Update your personal contact info](./contact_infos.md)

### Your Uffizi' Gallery

Modestly we consider your works all remarkable works, and we help you
to participate storing a selection of them and send what you want
to our hosted contest in a simple way.  
One at time you can upload, assign a title and some other infos.
At first contest participation automatic and human based check
are registered to validate your works for next contest participation,
check one valid most.
